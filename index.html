About Me

Bridging Psychology & Technology

I'm Ross Crawford, a UX/UI focused designer with a background in psychology and hands-on front end developments.

My path into design began with a Bachelor’s degree in Psychology, where I developed a strong interest in how people think, make decisions, and interact with systems. That foundation was strengthened through my work as a mental health support worker, where empathy, clear communication, and problem-solving under pressure were essential every day.

These experiences shape how I approach UX/UI design. I think deeply about users, accessibility, and clarity, not just how something looks, but how it feels to use. I design with intention, and I build with an understanding of real technical constraints.

I’m especially motivated by work that creates inclusive, intuitive digital experiences, and I enjoy collaborating closely with developers to ensure design intent carries through to implementation.

I’m currently seeking UX/UI design opportunities where I can continue developing my craft, contribute meaningfully to user-centred projects, and grow within a thoughtful, collaborative team.
