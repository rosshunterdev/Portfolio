About Me

Bridging Psychology & Technology

I'm Ross Crawford, a software development student with a unique background that combines technical skills with deep human understanding. My journey started with a Bachelor's degree in Psychology, where I developed a fascination with how people think, learn, and interact with the world around them.

Working as a mental health support worker taught me invaluable lessons about empathy, communication under pressure, and problem-solving in high-stakes situations. These experiences shape how I approach software development—always keeping the end user's needs, accessibility, and mental wellbeing at the forefront of design decisions.

Now, I'm channeling this unique perspective into creating technology that's not just functional, but genuinely helpful and human-centered. Whether it's building accessible web applications, designing engaging game experiences, or solving complex technical challenges, I bring both analytical thinking and emotional intelligence to every project.

Currently seeking opportunities in junior development roles, game development, and IT recruitment where I can contribute my blend of technical expertise and interpersonal skills.
