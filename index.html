About

I have a psychology degree and I'm finishing a software development diploma. That combination isn't accidental.

I'm building tools for people who've been underserved by technology. A lot of software is built by people who've never sat across from someone in crisis, or tried to navigate a system that wasn't designed for how their brain works. I have. That's what I bring to this work.

My background is in mental health and disability support. My projects reflect that — task management built around how ADHD brains actually work, tools for support workers that address gaps I saw firsthand, websites designed to make vulnerable people feel safe enough to take the next step.
